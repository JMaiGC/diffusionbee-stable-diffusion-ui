DiffusionBee - Stable Diffusion App for MacOS

DiffusionBee is the easiest way to generate AI art on your computer with Stable Diffusion. Comes with a one-click installer. No dependencies or technical knowledge needed.

  • Intel 64 Bit

    Good with any intel based Mac.

  • Apple Silicon

    Good with M1 / M2 etc. processors.

  • M1 Legacy Version - Runs slower but
    produces better images.



Features:

1) Runs locally on your computer
2) Clean and easy to use UI
3) One click installer
4) Image to image
5) In-painting
6) Out-painting
7) Upscaling images
8) Negative prompts and advanced prompt options
9) Data privacy - nothing is sent to the cloud ( unless you chose to share an image )
10) One-click install
